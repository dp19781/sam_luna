About Sam Luna

Hello and thanks for checking out my work! My name is Sam and I am a full-time, proud educator in San Jose. I am a self-taught photographer with years of experience. I worked for a couple of corporate studios during college and I picked up my own camera from there. I love capturing moments to look back on and smile about. Working closely with clients for portraits is something special to me so you get the full experience you want and hope for. I specialize in outdoor photography sessions and family portraits, using both digital and film.
